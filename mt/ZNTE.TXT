  >> 2 <<  REQUIREMENTS
-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-

  Hardware:

  o Tatung Einstein TC-01 with 64K of
    base RAM and 16K of video RAM.

  o MESS v0.138 (note: it doesn't work
    with versions 0.139 upto 0.146)

  >> 3 <<  THE GAME
-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-

  Welcome to ZOMBIE NEAR, fight for
  your life inside laboratories filled
  with zombies with a taste for your
  flesh, you have unlimited ammo for
  your handgun.

  Find the keys to open access to more
  floors of the laboratory, take life
  packs to recover your energy and save
  some scientists for extra points.

  There are rumors about zombie bosses
  waiting to crush you!

  Player 1 is controlled with arrow
  keys and space to shoot.

  Player 2 is controller with WASD
  keys and HT to shoot.

  Wait in title screen to see story.
